Alessandro E. C. Redondi

Politecnico di Milano

Alessandro Redondi is an Associate Professor in Telecommunications at the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering of Politecnico di Milano. His research interests include wireless networks, Internet of Things and Network Data Analysis. He is COMPACT coordinator and Principal Investigator.

Martino Trevisan

University of Trieste

Martino Trevisan is an Assistant Professor in Computer Engineering at the Department of Engineering and Architecture of the University of Trieste. His interests are network measurements and big data methodologies for Web and Internet analysis. He is the responsible of the COMPACT research unit at the University of Trieste.

Fabio Palmese

Politecico di Milano

Fabio Palmese received the M.S. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Politecnico di Milano, Milan, Italy, in 2020, where he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. His research activities focus on network traffic analysis and Internet of Things topics. From September 2023 he is a Visiting PhD at University College of London in the NetFort group, where his main research focus is security and privacy for IoT devices.

Giovanni Baccichet

Politecnico di Milano

Giovanni Baccichet is currently a Research Fellow at the Department of Electronics, Information, and Bioengineering of Politecnico di Milano. He obtained his MSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Politecnico di Milano, with a specialization in wireless networks and cybersecurity. Additionally, he holds a BSc in Information and Communication Engineering from the University of Trento, with a focus on communication system design. His research interests primarily revolve around networks, privacy, and security.

Damiano Ravalico

Università di Trieste

Damiano Ravalico completed his studies in October 2023 when he obtained his master's degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering from the University of Trieste, Italy. The following month he started his PhD at the same university. His main areas of interest are network measurements and cyber security.

Gabriele Merlach

Università di Trieste

Gabriele Merlach currently serves as a Research Assistant at the University of Trieste, affiliated with the Engineering and Architecture department. He holds an MSc in Computer Science and Engineering from the same university, specializing in the passive analysis and characterization of network traffic. His research focuses on the intersection of big data analysis and network traffic measurement.
